## What we collect from children, and how

- **At application:** we collect a parent's email at Stage 1. In Stage 2, the parent provides the child's name, grade, and school, and the child completes a non-identifying section that the parent reviews and submits. A student's own email is never collected.
- **After enrollment:** tier and project records, mentor hours, timeline, and, with separate consent, community participation and any public profile or published work.
- **From the parent:** contact and guardian details, the signed enrollment and consent form, and payment status (payment processing is handled by Stripe; we store status only, no card data).

We do not condition a child's participation on disclosing more information than is reasonably necessary (§ 312.7). A student who never makes anything public can complete every tier.
